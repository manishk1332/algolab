# TOPIK I 시험 문제 지시어 Complete Master Guide (Question Headings Guide)

This guide covers all question types, instruction headings, rubrics, and patterns that appear in the **TOPIK I (Listening & Reading)** examinations. Understanding these headings instantly allows you to know what strategy to use during the test.

---

## Part 1: 듣기 평가 지시어 (Listening Test Headings)

### 1. [1~4번] 대답 고르기 (Choosing the Correct Answer)
> **국문 지시어:** 다음을 듣고 보기와 같이 물음에 맞는 대답을 고르십시오.
> **English:** Listen to the following and choose the correct answer to the question as in the example.
>
> **어휘 및 문법 해설 (Word & Grammar Breakdown):**
> * **다음** (da-um): Next / following
> * **을** (eul): Object marker
> * **듣고** (deut-go): Listen and... (듣다 + 고)
> * **보기** (bo-gi): Example
> * **와 같이** (wa gat-i): Like / as / together with
> * **물음** (mu-reum): Question
> * **에** (e): To / at / in (particle)
> * **맞는** (man-neun): Correct / fitting (맞다 + 는)
> * **대답** (dae-dap): Answer / response
> * **을** (eul): Object marker
> * **고르십시오** (go-reu-sip-si-o): Please choose (고르다 + 십시오)
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> Speaker A asks a direct question (e.g., "Do you have an umbrella?", "What time is it?", "What did you eat?"). Match the question type (Yes/No vs. Who/What/When/Where/Why/How) with a logically matching answer.

---

### 2. [5~6번] 이어지는 말 고르기 (Choosing the Next Logical Response)
> **국문 지시어:** 다음을 듣고 보기와 같이 이어지는 말을 고르십시오.
> **English:** Listen to the following and choose the statement that follows logically as in the example.
>
> **어휘 및 문법 해설 (Word & Grammar Breakdown):**
> * **이어지는** (i-eo-ji-neun): Connecting / continuing / following (이어지다 + 는)
> * **말** (mal): Words / speech / statement
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> Speaker A gives a daily greeting, apology, thank you, or simple request (e.g., "Thank you so much", "Please wait a moment", "Goodbye"). Pick the standard social response (e.g., "Not at all", "Understood", "Goodbye").

---

### 3. [7~10번] 장소 찾기 (Identifying the Location)
> **국문 지시어:** 여기는 어디입니까? 보기와 같이 알맞은 것을 고르십시오.
> **English:** Where is this place? Choose the correct option as in the example.
>
> **어휘 및 문법 해설 (Word & Grammar Breakdown):**
> * **여기** (yeo-gi): Here / this place
> * **는** (neun): Topic marker
> * **어디** (eo-di): Where
> * **입니까** (im-ni-kka): Is it? (question form of 이다)
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> Listen for key location-specific keywords (e.g., "medicine/throat" = 약국 Pharmacy, "bus coming" = 정류장 Bus stop, "camera/sit down" = 사진관 Photo studio, "pants/size" = 옷 가게 Clothing store).

---

### 4. [11~14번] 주제 찾기 (Identifying the Topic)
> **국문 지시어:** 다음은 무엇에 대해 말하고 있습니까? 보기와 같이 알맞은 것을 고르십시오.
> **English:** What are they talking about? Choose the correct option as in the example.
>
> **어휘 및 문법 해설 (Word & Grammar Breakdown):**
> * **무엇** (mu-eot): What
> * **에 대해** (e dae-hae): About / regarding
> * **말하고 있습니까** (mal-ha-go it-seum-ni-kka): Are they talking? (말하다 + 고 있다 + ㅂ니까)
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> Identify the common umbrella category for the words mentioned in the dialogue (e.g., "Japan/Vietnam" = 나라 Country, "doctor/company" = 직업 Occupation, "soccer/basketball" = 운동 Sports, "spring/autumn" = 계절 Season).

---

### 5. [15~16번] 그림 고르기 (Selecting the Appropriate Picture)
> **국문 지시어:** 다음을 듣고 가장 알맞은 그림을 고르십시오.
> **English:** Listen to the following and choose the most appropriate picture.
>
> **어휘 및 문법 해설 (Word & Grammar Breakdown):**
> * **가장** (ga-jang): Most
> * **그림** (geu-rim): Picture / drawing
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> Pay close attention to spatial directions (in front of the door, on the left, top shelf) or actions being performed (hanging a frame, carrying a big bag, ordering food at a counter).

---

### 6. [17~21번] 내용 일치 확인 (Matching Dialogue Content)
> **국문 지시어:** 다음을 듣고 보기와 같이 대화 내용과 같은 것을 고르십시오.
> **English:** Listen to the following and choose the option that matches the content of the conversation.
>
> **어휘 및 문법 해설 (Word & Grammar Breakdown):**
> * **대화** (dae-hwa): Dialogue / conversation
> * **내용** (nae-yong): Content
> * **과** (gwa): With / same as (particle)
> * **같은** (ga-teun): Same / matching (같다 + 은)
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> Read the four answer choices *before* the audio plays. Cross out choices that contradict the dialogue (e.g., who did what, when it happened, or what someone plans to do).

---

### 7. [22~24번] 여자의/남자의 중심 생각 (Main Thought / Opinion)
> **국문 지시어:** 다음을 듣고 여자의/남자의 중심 생각을 고르십시오.
> **English:** Listen to the following and choose the main thought/opinion of the woman/man.
>
> **어휘 및 문법 해설 (Word & Grammar Breakdown):**
> * **중심 생각** (jung-sim saeng-gak): Main thought / core opinion / key mindset
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> Look for opinions, reasons, or key assertions (e.g., "I like pencils because...", "Beds must be seen in person before buying", "I want to order one more of the same"). Focus on *why* the targeted speaker expresses their opinion.

---

### 8. [25~30번] 복합 지문 문제 (Long Passages / Announcements / Interviews)
> **국문 지시어:**
> * **[25번/27번]** 여자가/두 사람이 왜 이 이야기를 하고 있는지 / 무엇에 대해 이야기하고 있는지 고르십시오. (Purpose / Topic)
> * **[26번/28번/30번]** 들은 내용과 같은 것을 고르십시오. (Fact checking)
> * **[29번]** 여자가 ~가 된 이유 / ~을 한 이유를 고르십시오. (Reason)
>
> **어휘 및 문법 해설 (Word & Grammar Breakdown):**
> * **왜** (wae): Why
> * **이유** (i-yu): Reason
> * **들으십시오** (deu-reu-sip-si-o): Please listen
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> Passages 25–30 play twice! On the first play, answer the overall question (Purpose/Topic/Reason). On the second play, verify the factual detail statement (내용과 같은 것).

---

## Part 2: 읽기 평가 지시어 (Reading Test Headings)

### 1. [31~33번] 무엇에 대한 내용인가? (Identifying Topic)
> **국문 지시어:** 무엇에 대한 내용입니까? <보기>와 같이 알맞은 것을 고르십시오.
> **English:** What is this content about? Choose the correct option as in the example.
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> Short 2-sentence items. Find the common theme (e.g., "Apple, Pear" = 과일 Fruit; "Rain, Cold" = 날씨 Weather; "Sister, Brother" = 가족 Family).

---

### 2. [34~39번] 빈칸에 들어갈 말 (Filling in the Blank)
> **국문 지시어:** <보기>와 같이 ( )에 들어갈 말로 가장 알맞은 것을 고르십시오.
> **English:** Choose the most appropriate word/expression to fill in the parentheses ( ).
>
> **어휘 및 문법 해설 (Word & Grammar Breakdown):**
> * **빈칸** (bin-kan): Blank space / parentheses ( )
> * **들어갈** (deul-eo-gal): To enter / fit into (들어가다 + ㄹ)
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> Pay attention to context clues (e.g., "Shoes are ( ). My feet hurt." -> 작습니다 small; "Many tasks at work. So ( ) busy." -> 아주 very). Pay attention to particles (-의, -을, -에) or connective endings (-어서, -지만, -면서).

---

### 3. [40~42번] 맞지 않는 것 고르기 (Choosing What Does NOT Match)
> **국문 지시어:** 다음을 읽고 맞지 않는 것을 고르십시오.
> **English:** Read the following and choose the option that does NOT match / is incorrect.
>
> **어휘 및 문법 해설 (Word & Grammar Breakdown):**
> * **맞지 않는** (mat-ji an-neun): Not correct / NOT matching (맞다 + 지 않다 + 는)
>
> ⚠️ **CAUTION:** Watch out! 3 options are TRUE and 1 option is FALSE. You must select the FALSE statement! Check dates, times, prices, and locations carefully.

---

### 4. [43~45번] 내용이 같은 것 고르기 (Choosing Matching Content)
> **국문 지시어:** 다음을 읽고 내용이 같은 것을 고르십시오.
> **English:** Read the following and choose the option that has the same content.
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> Factual match question. Compare each option line-by-line with the passage. Watch out for subtle traps (e.g., changing "this month" to "next month", or "with a friend" to "alone").

---

### 5. [46~48번] 중심 내용 고르기 (Identifying Main Idea)
> **국문 지시어:** 다음을 읽고 중심 내용을 고르십시오.
> **English:** Read the following and choose the main idea / core message.
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> The main idea is usually expressed in the final sentence or summarizes the writer's overall feeling/attitude (e.g., "I enjoy learning dance", "I want to replace my old AC", "I really like this convenience store"). Do not pick simple detail statements!

---

### 6. [49~50, 51~52, 53~54, 55~56, 61~62, 63~64, 65~66, 67~68, 69~70번] 지문 세트 문제 (Reading Passage Sets)
> **공통 질문 유형 (Common Question Types in Passage Sets):**
>
> 1. **㉠에 들어갈 말로 가장 알맞은 것을 고르십시오.**
>    * Choose the most appropriate grammar connector or phrase for blank ㉠ (e.g., 그리고, 하지만, 그래서, 또, -았지만, -으면서).
>
> 2. **윗글의 내용과 같은 / 맞지 않는 것을 고르십시오.**
>    * Choose the statement that matches / does not match the passage above.
>
> 3. **무엇에 대한 내용인지 맞는 것을 고르십시오.**
>    * Choose the correct overall topic of the text (e.g., "Things you can do with flour", "Things you can do at the festival").
>
> 4. **왜 윗글을 썼는지 맞는 것을 고르십시오.**
>    * Choose the purpose of writing the text (e.g., "To announce pool cleaning days", "To introduce a new bicycle parking lot").
>
> 5. **윗글의 내용으로 알 수 있는 것을 고르십시오.**
>    * Choose what can be inferred/deduced from the passage.

---

### 7. [57~58번] 문장 순서 배열하기 (Sentence Ordering)
> **국문 지시어:** 다음을 순서에 맞게 배열한 것을 고르십시오.
> **English:** Choose the option that arranges the following sentences in the correct order.
>
> **어휘 및 문법 해설 (Word & Grammar Breakdown):**
> * **순서** (sun-seo): Order / sequence
> * **배열한 것** (bae-yeol-han geot): Arranged items (배열하다 + ㄴ 것)
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> 1. Look at the first sentence choices (usually starts with (가) or (라)).
> 2. The first sentence is a general topic statement (introduces the situation).
> 3. Look for transition words like **그래서** (so), **하지만** (however), **이 때문에** (because of this), **그때** (at that time), **그것** (that).
> 4. Ensure logical flow: Cause -> Effect -> Result/Conclusion.

---

### 8. [59~60번] 문장 위치 찾기 (Inserting a Sentence into the Text)
> **국문 지시어:** 다음 문장이 들어갈 곳으로 가장 알맞은 것을 고르십시오.
> **English:** Choose the most appropriate place where the following sentence should enter.
>
> 💡 **시험 풀이 전략 (Strategy):**
> Read the given sentence carefully and look for reference pronouns (이, 그, 저), conjunctions (그래서, 하지만), or time flow (어느 날, 그때) that anchor it to the surrounding text.
